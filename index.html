About Me

I love data and technology, and how they can be used to solve real-world problems. I have a strong eye for quality and detail, and I enjoy working in teams to deliver high-quality products. My earlier experiences in software QA—before my career gap—shaped me to be adaptable, resilient, and a fast learner.
